Our Mission

Making AI Skills Accessible to Every Working Professional in India

The world is changing fast. AI is reshaping every industry — data, marketing, finance, operations. But most upskilling options in India are either too expensive, too theoretical, or simply not backed by any real outcome commitment.

Learn24 was built to close that gap. We combine IIT and IIM-level academic rigour with practical, project-based learning — and we stand behind every student with a 100% job placement guarantee.

We believe that where you start should never define where you end up. A written job guarantee is our promise to every student who walks through our door.

😤 The Problem We Saw

Thousands of working professionals were being left behind — taking generic online courses with no mentorship, no projects, no placement support, and no guarantee. They were spending money and time with nothing to show for it.

💡 The Learn24 Solution

We built a model where every course is designed by IIT/IIM alumni, delivered by industry practitioners, supported by a dedicated Course Manager, and backed by a written 100% job placement guarantee. Outcome first, always.

🎯 Our Promise to You

Complete our programme. Get placed — or receive a full refund. No fine print. No excuses. That's the Learn24 commitment.

The Course Manager model is designed
We pioneered the Course Manager framework — every programme led by an IIT/IIM alumnus overseeing curriculum, trainers, and placement. Our first pilot batch of 20 students achieved 100% placement.

2024 — AI Integration
Courses rebuilt AI-first
Every curriculum was rebuilt from scratch to integrate AI tools at every step — not as an add-on, but as the core of how students learn, practise, and work. Three AI-powered courses launched.

2025 — 120+ Placed
The guarantee holds strong
Over 120 working professionals placed across India's top companies. Zero refund requests. The model works — and we're just getting started.

A Story Built on One Unbreakable Promise

Learn24 didn't start as a business. It started as a conversation between three alumni who kept watching talented professionals get stuck — not because they lacked potential, but because they lacked access to the right training and the right doors.

We knew that the traditional edtech model was broken. Pre-recorded videos, zero mentorship, certificates that hiring managers ignored. The industry needed something different.

Today, every cohort is supervised by an IIT or IIM alumnus, and every graduate carries a written job guarantee in their pocket.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Four principles that shape every decision we make — from curriculum design to placement support.

Outcome First

We measure our success by one metric — did our student get placed? Everything we do, from curriculum to mentorship to mock interviews, is in service of that single outcome.
