Eligibility Conditions for Placement Guarantee

To qualify for our placement guarantee and potential refund, students must fulfill all of the following conditions throughout the course duration:

  • 1 90% Lecture Attendance — Maintain a minimum of 90% attendance in all scheduled live lectures and sessions.
  • 2 Timely Assignment Completion — Submit all assignments before their respective deadlines without exception.
  • 3 Timely Task Completion — Complete all assigned tasks within the specified timeframe as directed by instructors.
  • 4 Timely Project Completion — Deliver all course projects on or before the assigned deadlines.
  • 5 Apply to All Referred Jobs — Submit applications to every job opportunity shared by the Learn24 placement team.
  • 6 Accept All Valid Offers — Do not reject any legitimate job offers received through our placement process.
Important Note

Failure to comply with any of the above conditions will disqualify you from the placement guarantee and refund eligibility. All conditions are mandatory and will be tracked throughout your learning journey.

Assignment & Project Completion

All coursework must be completed within the specified deadlines to maintain your eligibility for the placement guarantee:

  • • Assignments: Weekly or module-based assignments must be submitted before the deadline.
  • • Tasks: Practical tasks assigned during or after lectures must be completed as per instructor guidelines.
  • • Projects: Capstone and mini-projects are essential for your portfolio and must be delivered on time.

Late submissions will be marked as incomplete and may affect your placement guarantee eligibility. Extensions are granted only in exceptional circumstances with prior approval from the academic team.

Placement Process & Job Applications

Our placement team actively connects eligible students with hiring companies. To remain eligible for the placement guarantee:

  • • Apply to All Jobs: You must apply to every job opportunity shared by our placement team through official channels.
  • • No Offer Rejections: If you receive a legitimate job offer through our placement process, you must accept it. Rejecting valid offers will void your placement guarantee.
  • • Active Participation: Attend all scheduled interviews, mock interviews, and placement preparation sessions.
What Counts as a Valid Offer

A legitimate job offer includes any full-time position in your field of study that meets industry-standard compensation and provides a genuine opportunity for professional growth and career development.

Refund Policy

100% Refund Guarantee

If you fulfill all eligibility conditions and do not receive an offer letter within 100 days of course completion, you are eligible for a full refund of your course fee.

To claim your refund, ensure:

  • ✓ You maintained 90% or higher lecture attendance throughout the course.
  • ✓ All assignments, tasks, and projects were submitted on time.
  • ✓ You applied to every job opportunity shared by our placement team.
  • ✓ You did not reject any legitimate job offer received through our process.
  • ✓ 100 days have passed since your official course completion date without receiving an offer.

Refund requests must be submitted within 15 days after the 100-day period ends. Our team will verify your compliance with all conditions before processing the refund.
